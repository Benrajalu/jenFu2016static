About

IDEAS

are my thing. Understanding unmet needs and problem solving. Behind every storyboard, site, and app, there should be a supporting campaign or solid concept. Working across integrated platforms provides consistency, and flow, of how people engage. My background in information design, gives me the ability to be hands on from hand drawn scamps, producing sitemaps, prioritising data, creating user journeys to support complex navigation, to Mac visuals for client presentations.

Communication

is key. Freelance trains you to be a faster thinker and a precise director, yet I supportively encourage others, to move away from desks and screens. Go get inspired. Be involved and ask. Absorb everything. Locally and globally. Your audience are everyday people. Trends and behavioural patterns change and adapt, so immerse yourself and stay ahead. Relate to their needs, aspirations, fears and only then you can challenge a brief, and communicate better solutions.
